Privacy Policy

We Respect Your Privacy

Your personal data is invaluable, and we take its protection seriously. We're committed to maintaining your trust by being transparent about how we handle your information and providing you with meaningful control over your data.

01

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly—such as your name, email, phone number, and message content—when you submit forms or contact us. Additionally, we gather technical data like IP address, browser type, device information, and browsing patterns for analytics and service improvement purposes.

Data Collection
02

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us respond to inquiries, process service requests, enhance your user experience, and deliver relevant updates. We respect your preferences and provide easy options to opt out of promotional communications at any time through our unsubscribe feature.

Data Usage
03

Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption, secure servers, and multi-layered security protocols to protect your data. While we continuously work to ensure maximum security, we acknowledge that no online system is completely immune to risks and remain transparent about our limitations.

Security
04

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. Information is only shared with trusted service providers (hosting, analytics, payment processing) under strict confidentiality agreements, or when legally required to comply with regulatory obligations.

Data Sharing

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. If you wish to exercise these rights or have data-related requests, please contact us at info@brandsway.com.
